What the Scholarship Will Do:

The Bland Family Opportunity Scholarship will provide annual financial support to Elon Law students with verified financial need who are pursuing a Juris Doctor degree.

The fund is permanent and may grow through future contributions from the Bland Family or others. It is invested alongside the University’s endowment, with annual distributions supporting new students each year.

Recipients are selected by Elon Law’s admissions and financial aid teams, in consultation with the dean, in alignment with University standards.
